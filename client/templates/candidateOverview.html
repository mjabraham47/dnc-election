Candidate Endorsement

Step 1

Read through the candidate profiles. Click on each candidate's name on the left to see their bio, personal statement and platform.

Step 2

Endorse your choice for DNC chair by clicking on the 'Endorse' button. You can only endorse one candidate. Information collected is used to help us connect you to your electors.

Step 3

Check out your list of electors and contact a few of them to let them know who you'd like them to vote for as next DNC chair on Feb 26.
